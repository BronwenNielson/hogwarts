Our Facilities

As well as state of the art classrooms, we also have many facilites to make your life more enjoyable here at Hogwarts.

Madam Pince keeps out library stocked with tomes of magic from around the world, in every subject available. Our restricted section is also accesible to older students, to learn more of the dangers of the darker side of magic.

Whether you're a spectator or a player, our famous Quidditch pitch is the place to be during the house tournament.
