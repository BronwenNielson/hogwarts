About Us

Britain's Only Magical Academy

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, often shortened to Hogwarts, is a Scottish wizarding school located in the Scottish Highlands. We accept any magical student residing in the islands of Great Britain and Ireland at the age of eleven years old at the start of the incoming term for enrolment. Established around the 10th century, Hogwarts is considered to be one of the finest magical institutions in the wizarding world. The school's motto is Draco Dormiens Nunquam Titillandus (Draco Dormiens Nvnqvam Titillandvs), which, translated from Latin, means "Never tickle a sleeping dragon".
